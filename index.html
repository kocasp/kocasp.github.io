THIS IS ME

I am a web developer and amatour graphic designer. I spend my days with my hands in many different areas of web development from back end programming (Ruby on Rails) to front end engineering (HTML, CSS, and jQuery/Javascript), visual design and animations. I’m extremely passionate about web development and design in all it’s forms and helping businesses and artists build and improve their online presence. Aside from web development, I enjoy spending my time with my friends, traveling, making films and playing sports.

OweZone

Owe zone is my first-ever Ruby on Rails application from 2013. The idea is to manage common spendings on a trip or event. App is analysing who was paying for goods and who was using them and then calculating who owes money to whom. Currently its a web-only responsive app but probably will be ported to native app soon.
